Guess That Country


You have 5 attempts to guess the name of a country. Throughout these 5 attempts you will obtain 4 hints that will help you figure out the country. Try to remember the hints, as after each guess, the hint will be replaced with another. There are 3 buttons that you can use. The button "guess" is for you to submit your answer. The button "clear" will clear whatever you typed into the textbox. The button "new" will restart the game and give a new country for you to guess. Good luck and have fun!
